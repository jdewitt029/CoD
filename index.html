CoD Memories (2020)

Video Editing
1920x1080
0:21

This edit is my first attempt at making a video edit in After Effects. Call of Duty trickshotting is something that I spent a lot of time on. Montages were a big part of that community and I was always amazed at how the montages were edited. This work was a way for me to reminisce on the fun days I spent playing that game. It also allowed me to do the thing that once amzed me. I tried to create harmony between the video and the music with the effects that I used.
